About Me

Jordan Boggs is new to web development, but no stranger to the web. From 2012-2017, she worked for the University of Pennsylvania's Online Learning department as an instructional technologist and head of media production. Currently, she works as a video editor, web assistant, and general helper-person at Motion Pixel Lab in Colorado Springs. She hopes to use her past experience in elearning and media to inform her future work as a developer.
